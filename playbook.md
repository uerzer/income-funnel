# Funnel Playbook - Async Info Product Business

## Russell Brunson Model (Simplified)

### Value Ladder
1. **Bait (Free)** - Lead magnet that solves a specific pain
2. **Tripwire ($7-27)** - Low-risk entry point  
3. **Core Offer ($97-297)** - Main transformation product
4. **Profit Maximizer ($497-1997)** - High-ticket/backend

## Our Niche: Remote Income Automation
Target: People who want to make money online without skills/coding

### Lead Magnet: "7-Day Remote Income Blueprint"
- Free PDF/Notion template
- 7 specific methods to make first $1000 online
- Zero coding, zero experience needed
- Delivered via email

### Tripwire: "Automation Toolkit ($17)"
- 50+ automation scripts (meals, bills, data entry)
- Step-by-step setup videos
- Notion workspace template
- 7-day money back guarantee

### Core Offer: "Income Automation Academy ($197)"
- 6-week course
- Build 3 income streams in automation
- Templates, scripts, workflows
- Private community (Discord/Telegram)
- Weekly Q&A recordings (async)

### Profit Maximizer: "Done-With-You Setup ($997)"
- I build their first 2 automations
- Custom scripts for their needs
- 30 days support
- Money-back if no results

## Traffic Strategy
1. **Organic**: Tiktok/YouTube shorts showing results
2. **Paid**: Meta ads to cold traffic ($20/day test)
3. **Partners**: Affiliate program (40% commission)

## Tech Stack
- Landing page: Static HTML (host anywhere)
- Email: Mailchimp/ConvertKit free tier
- Payment: **Polar.sh** (4% + 40¢, Merchant of Record, global tax compliance) or Gumroad
- Delivery: Notion/Email/Polar
- Community: Telegram/Discord (free)

## Funnel Flow
```
Ad/Social → Landing Page → Email Opt-in
                        ↓
               Thank You + Tripwire Offer (OTF)
                        ↓
               Email Sequence (5 days)
                        ↓
               Core Offer Pitch
                        ↓
               Profit Maximizer (application)
```
