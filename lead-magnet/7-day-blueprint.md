## Day 1: AI Data Entry ($15-25/hr)

**What it is:** Companies have messy data. Spreadsheets with errors, duplicates, wrong formats. They'll pay you to clean it up.

**The AI shortcut:**
1. Get data from client (CSV, spreadsheet)
2. Upload to ChatGPT/Claude
3. Prompt: "Clean this data. Remove duplicates, fix formatting, standardize dates."
4. Review output
5. Deliver clean file

**Time:** 30 mins learning, 10 mins per file

**Getting clients:**
- Upwork: Search "data cleaning", "data entry", "spreadsheet cleanup"
- Fiverr: Create gig "I will clean and organize your data with AI"
- Cold email: Find e-commerce stores, offer to clean their product data

**Earnings potential:** $100-500/week

## Day 2: Content Repurposing ($20-50 per piece)

**What it is:** Podcasters make content. They need it turned into blog posts, tweets, LinkedIn posts. You do the conversion.

**The AI shortcut:**
1. Get podcast/video link from client
2. Use AI to transcribe (Whisper, Otter.ai free tier)
3. Prompt: "Turn this transcript into a 1000-word blog post about [topic]"
4. Format and add headers
5. Deliver

**Getting clients:**
- Find podcasts in niches you understand
- Email: "I can turn each episode into 3 blog posts and 10 tweets"
- Charge: $50 per blog post, $25 per 10 tweets

**Earnings potential:** $200-800/week

---

## Day 3: Email Inbox Management ($20-35/hr)

**What it is:** Executives and business owners are drowning in email. You organize, draft replies, flag what matters.

**The AI shortcut:**
1. Get inbox access (Gmail delegation)
2. Use AI to draft responses
3. Prompt: "Draft a professional reply to this email"
4. Review and send (or save as draft for approval)

**Getting clients:**
- LinkedIn: Search "executive" + "CEO" in your city
- Message: "I help executives reclaim 5+ hours/week by managing their inbox"
- Start at $20/hr, raise to $35 after 2 weeks

**Earnings potential:** $400-1200/week (20-40 hrs)

---

## Day 4: Appointment Setting ($10-20 per meeting booked)

**What it is:** Sales teams need meetings. You find prospects, send sequences, book calls.

**The AI shortcut:**
1. Get list of target companies
2. Use AI to write personalized outreach
3. Prompt: "Write a cold email to [prospect] referencing their recent [news/achievement]"
4. Send via their CRM or your email tool
5. Follow up till they book

**Getting clients:**
- LinkedIn: Find salespeople posting about quotas
- Offer: "I book 3-5 qualified meetings per week on autopilot"
- Charge per meeting booked (they only pay for results)

**Earnings potential:** $300-1000/week

## Day 5-6: Pick Your Specialty

Choose one that fits your interest:

**Customer Support ($15-25/hr)**
- Answer tickets using AI-drafted responses
- E-commerce stores always need this
- Use to train the AI on their brand voice

**Newsletter Operations ($200-500/mo per client)**
- Build newsletters in Beehiiv/ConvertKit
- Write content with AI
- Clients: coaches, consultants, thought leaders

**Social Media Management ($200-800/mo per client)**
- Schedule posts, write captions
- Use AI for bulk content creation
- Small businesses need this desperately

## Day 7: Scale Your First Income Stream

**Don't do everything. Pick ONE.**

Master it. Get 2-3 clients. Then add a second service.

**Scaling formula:**
1. Land 1 client, do great work
2. Ask for testimonial + referral
3. Raise price 20%
4. Land client #2
5. Repeat until maxed out
6. Raise prices, become selective
7. Hire help or add service
